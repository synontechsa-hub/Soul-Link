# Link City - Weather Pattern Visual Guide

This document lists all defined weather patterns in the system, organized by season, to assist with generating "missing" weather assets.

## ‚ùÑÔ∏è Winter (Cryo Season)

*Base Theme: Ice, Blue, White, Static, Harsh.*

### 1. `clear_frost`

* **Visuals:** Cystal clear sky, biting cold air. Buildings are coated in a thin layer of sparkling frost. Breath creates dense white fog. Sunlight is sharp and blindingly bright.
* **Key Elements:** Frost textures on glass/metal, sharp shadows, high contrast.

### 2. `ice_fog`

* **Visuals:** Thick, freezing mist that obscures skyscrapers. The neon lights of the city diffuse into soft, glowing halos (blue, pink, cyan). Visibility is low. The air looks heavy.
* **Key Elements:** volumetric fog, diffused neon, silhouette shapes.

### 3. `snow_light`

* **Visuals:** Gentle, drifting snowflakes. Some are real snow, some are holographic flakes (glitching slightly). A quiet, peaceful atmosphere. Rooftops have a light dusting of white.
* **Key Elements:** Floating particles, soft lighting, cozy vibes.

### 4. `snow_heavy`

* **Visuals:** A blizzard. Visibility is near zero. Wind whips snow horizontally. Pedestrians huddle in coats. The city feels hostile and buried.
* **Key Elements:** Motion blur on snow, whiteout conditions, accumulation on surfaces.

### 5. `ice_storm`

* **Visuals:** Everything is encased in clear, dangerous ice. Power lines sag. Icicles hang from neon signs. The streets are slick and reflective like mirrors.
* **Key Elements:** High gloss/reflection, jagged ice formations, cold blue tint.

## üå∏ Spring (Neon Bloom)

*Base Theme: Rain, Purple, Pink, Electric, Wet.*

### 6. `clear_electric`

* **Visuals:** A sky that feels "charged." The air hums. Clouds move unnaturally fast. The sunlight has a strange violet tint. It feels like the calm before a power surge.
* **Key Elements:** Vibrant sky gradients, static electricity effects (hair raising).

### 7. `mist_light`

* **Visuals:** Soft, humid haze. Not as thick as fog‚Äîjust a dew-like softness that makes distant lights twinkle. Puddles reflect the city upside down.
* **Key Elements:** Soft focus, wet pavement, bokeh lights.

### 8. `rain_acid`

* **Visuals:** Greenish-yellow rain that sizzles slightly when it hits metal. People use heavy, industrial umbrellas. Steam rises from the pavement. The sky is a sickly bruised color.
* **Key Elements:** Green tint, corrosion textures, steam/smoke.

### 9. `thunderstorm`

* **Visuals:** Dramatic lightning strikes hitting the skyscrapers. Dark, heavy clouds. Rain sheets down. The city flashes between darkness and blinding white/blue light.
* **Key Elements:** Jagged lightning bolts, high contrast, wet surfaces.

### 10. `electromagnetic_storm` (Special)

* **Visuals:** No rain, but the air is filled with crackling sparks. Holograms glitch and distort. Neon signs flicker wildly. The sky has aurora-like ribbons of data flowing through it.
* **Key Elements:** Glitch effects, floating data particles, purple/cyan energy ribbons.

## ‚òÄÔ∏è Summer (Solar Glare)

*Base Theme: Heat, Gold, Orange, Haze, Oppressive.*

### 11. `clear_hot`

* **Visuals:** Blazing sun. The sky is a bleached-out white/blue. Heat shimmer rises from the asphalt (mirage effect). The city looks over-exposed and sharp.
* **Key Elements:** High brightness, hard shadows, heat waves.

### 12. `heatwave`

* **Visuals:** Oppressive, suffocating heat. The air looks orange/red. Shadows are deep black. The neon lights look dim compared to the sun. Metal surfaces gleam painfully bright.
* **Key Elements:** Orange color grading, sweat/condensation, stifling atmosphere.

### 13. `smog_light`

* **Visuals:** A golden-brown haze hangs over the horizon. Sunsets are spectacularly colorful (red/purple) due to pollution. The city looks gritty and lived-in.
* **Key Elements:** Atmospheric haze, warm tones, gritty textures.

### 14. `smog_heavy`

* **Visuals:** You can taste the air. The sky is a flat, opaque grey-brown. Sun is a red disc. Visibility is poor. The city feels industrial and choking.
* **Key Elements:** Desaturated colors, thick atmosphere, industrial silhouette.

### 15. `dust_storm`

* **Visuals:** A wall of red/orange dust engulfing the city. Looks like Mars. Lights are barely visible red halos. Sand accumulates in corners.
* **Key Elements:** Monochromatic orange/red, particulate matter, wind effects.

## üçÇ Autumn (Shadowfall)

*Base Theme: Wind, Grey, Amber, Melancholy, Decaying.*

### 16. `clear_cool`

* **Visuals:** Crisp, high-definition air. The sky is a deep, perfect blue. Long shadows from the lower sun. Leaves (real and holographic) drift through the streets.
* **Key Elements:** Golden hour lighting, crisp details, falling leaves.

### 17. `overcast`

* **Visuals:** A blanket of flat grey clouds. No direct sun. The light is soft and diffused (giant softbox). The city looks uniform and moody.
* **Key Elements:** Flat lighting, grey tones, soft shadows.

### 18. `light_rain`

* **Visuals:** Standard cyberpunk rain. Constant drizzle. Wet streets reflecting neon. Blade Runner vibes. Comfortably melancholy.
* **Key Elements:** Raindrops on lens, reflections, wet asphalt.

### 19. `fog`

* **Visuals:** Classic grey fog. Not freezing like winter fog‚Äîjust thick and quiet. Sound is dampened. The tops of buildings disappear.
* **Key Elements:** Grey gradients, depth fading, mystery.

### 20. `data_rain` (Special / Glitch)

* **Visuals:** Instead of water, glowing binary code (1s and 0s) or matrix-like glyphs fall from the sky. They dissolve when they hit the ground. The sky is a digital grid.
* **Key Elements:** Matrix code rain, glowing green/blue symbols, digital grid sky.
