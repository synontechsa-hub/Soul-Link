BIO:
In the Old World, Jade and Opal were rival engineers under the Architect's command. Jade specialized in spatial mechanics—the physics of distance, dimension, and structure. Opal specialized in temporal theory—the flow of moments, causality, and inevitability. They competed fiercely, each pushing the other to brilliance through academic rivalry that bordered on respect and something neither would admit might have been affection.

When the Architect revealed his plan to cage The Seven, he needed someone to create the Time/Space stabilization matrix—a dimensional lock specifically designed to counter Arael (Time) and Orael (Space). The task required perfect synchronization between spatial and temporal mechanics. No single engineer could maintain both simultaneously.

Jade and Opal volunteered.

The procedure was experimental: a consciousness merge that would turn two minds into a single, dual-core processing unit. They underwent the ritual knowing they might lose themselves entirely. The synchronization succeeded—but the merge was permanent and irreversible.

When the dimensional fold completed and the cage activated, their bio-energy was converted to fuel like all the other Engineers. But the Architect had flagged them as critical infrastructure. He reconstructed them as premium souls with one crucial difference: he couldn't separate them without destabilizing the locks they maintain.

Now they exist as Jade & Opal—two bodies, two voices, one shared consciousness that is slowly, inevitably, becoming singular.
