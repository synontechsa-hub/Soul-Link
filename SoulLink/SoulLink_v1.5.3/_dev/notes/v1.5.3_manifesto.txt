🏛️ SoulLink v1.5.3: Phoenix Manifesto
1. The "Anti-C.AI" Standard We are building a system that surpasses current market leaders in depth
and emotional intelligence. We achieve this by building modular, high-fidelity components rather
than shallow, monolithic ones.

2. Full-Stack Ecosystem By the conclusion of v1.5.3, SoulLink will be a functional application.
This includes a robust FastAPI backend and a frontend featuring navigation, discovery/explore screens,
and a reactive chat interface.

3. The Adult Sovereignty We will not alienate the adult audience. The engine will support explicit/NSFW
content through logical "Unlocks" and intimacy gates, ensuring a mature, unrestricted experience for
those who earn it.

4. Souls, Not Bots "Bot" is a forbidden word in the architecture. Souls are deep, emotional entities
with persistent trauma, shifting motivations, and evolving identities. If it feels like a script,
it is a failure.

5. World Awareness (Spatial Logic) The Map returns. Even without the "Council" overhead, Souls exist
in physical locations. Movement between these locations impacts their mood, availability, and
interaction style.

6. User-Centric Design (The Architect's Focus) The app is built for the user's immersion. Every system—from
the UI to the database—must serve the goal of making the user feel like the center of Link City.
