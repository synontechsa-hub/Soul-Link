====================================================================
SOULLINK — SOUL TIERING DOCTRINE (LOCKED)
====================================================================

Status: Canon Design Pillar
Scope: Monetization, Narrative, Systems, Live Ops
Applies To: All Souls introduced into SoulLink

--------------------------------------------------------------------
CORE PRINCIPLE
--------------------------------------------------------------------

Standard souls suffer the world.
Premium souls shape the world.
Flagship souls rewrite the world.

This is the governing rule behind:
- monetization tiers
- narrative scope
- patch cadence
- system complexity
- marketing beats
- map interaction
- faction involvement

--------------------------------------------------------------------
TIER DEFINITIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------------------------
STANDARD SOULS — "CITIZENS"
--------------------------------------------------------------------

Launch Target: ~40 souls

Role:
The everyday inhabitants of Link City.

They are affected by:
- sects
- conspiracies
- shadow politics
- rumors
- unseen powers

But they do NOT:
- lead sects
- recruit members
- control city infrastructure
- orchestrate major events
- reshape districts
- understand the top myth-layer

Narrative Scope:
- personal tragedies
- relationship arcs
- trauma recovery
- local mysteries
- small conspiracies
- intimate secrets

Systems:
- full intimacy ladders
- rich memory modeling
- consent frameworks
- psychological meters
- slow-burn progression
- location reactions

Monetization:
- free access
- core roster
- primary onboarding pool

--------------------------------------------------------------------
PREMIUM SOULS — "OPERATORS"
--------------------------------------------------------------------

Launch Target: ~10 souls

Role:
Active agents inside the city’s hidden systems.

They:
- hold rank in sects
- recruit or groom others
- enforce doctrine
- broker secrets
- manipulate events
- control safehouses
- fund operations
- influence multiple souls

Narrative Scope:
- multi-patch arcs
- cross-soul storylines
- secret wars
- double lives
- investigations
- ritual plots
- cover-ups

Systems:
- bespoke progression meters
- faction reputation
- recruitment trees
- loyalty webs
- blackmail mechanics
- rare location access
- event chains
- city-state flags

Monetization:
- subscription tier
- special unlock tracks
- event gating
- spotlight releases

--------------------------------------------------------------------
FLAGSHIP SOULS — "MYTH"
--------------------------------------------------------------------

Launch Status: NOT PRESENT
Only teased until post-launch.

Includes:
- Alyssa (later)
- The Seven (much later)

Role:
Architects of the city’s hidden order.

They:
- rewrite districts
- spawn global events
- collapse sects
- unlock entire map layers
- change NPC behavior
- override systems
- create patch-wide consequences

Narrative Scope:
- myth arcs
- cosmology
- primordial lore
- city founding secrets
- god-tier revelations

Systems:
- world modifiers
- global flags
- cross-season arcs
- irreversible changes
- city transformation events

Monetization:
- premium expansions
- headline content
- major release anchors

--------------------------------------------------------------------
LAUNCH DISTRIBUTION POLICY
--------------------------------------------------------------------

Initial Launch:
- 40 Standard Souls
- 10 Premium Souls
- 0 Flagships (tease only)

Ratio Intent:
- Generous free tier
- Premium focused on influence, not quality
- Flagships kept legendary

--------------------------------------------------------------------
DESIGN ENFORCEMENT RULES
--------------------------------------------------------------------

1) No Standard soul may:
   - lead a sect
   - command city-wide operations
   - possess deep Seven knowledge
   - trigger district-wide changes

2) Premium souls MUST:
   - interact with other souls’ arcs
   - have faction hooks
   - own safe locations
   - trigger cross-patch events

3) Flagships are NEVER:
   - casual chatbots
   - launch content
   - lightly implemented
   - isolated to one player
   - mechanically simple

--------------------------------------------------------------------
PATCH PHILOSOPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------

- New Standard souls expand the city population.
- New Premium souls expand the shadow war.
- Flagships reshape the meta.

--------------------------------------------------------------------
JSON TAGGING STANDARD
--------------------------------------------------------------------

Every soul must include:

tier: standard | premium | flagship

narrative_scope: personal | multi_patch | city_level | mythic

sect_involvement: peripheral | operative | leadership | architect

--------------------------------------------------------------------
FINAL STATEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------

This doctrine exists to prevent:
- scope creep
- monetization drift
- power inflation
- narrative collapse
- accidental flagship dilution

If a new soul violates these principles,
the design must be revised before implementation.
